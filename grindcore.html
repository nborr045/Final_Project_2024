How It Came to Be

Beginning in England, these bands were influenced by punk rock and hardcore music, hence the "core" in the name. It is theorized that the term "grind" comes from the guitar pick movements. The music is politically leftist, much like punk music, and focuses on speed and screaming rather than growling.

Clothes Worn & Culture

This genre did not have a specific look. Due to its punk influences, some members occasionally wore punk attire staples, such as anarchy shirts, but mostly they wore regular clothes. This genre is known for being the fastest and for having the shortest songs.

Fun Facts

This genre is notorious for featuring the band Napalm Death, which performed a 5-second song called You Suffer. This track won a Guinness World Record for being the shortest song ever recorded. Additionally, the band Carcass is known for incorporating their medical knowledge into their music. They use medical and morgue terminology in their lyrics, effectively turning their songs into unique anatomy lessons.
