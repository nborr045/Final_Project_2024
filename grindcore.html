How It Came to Be

Beggining in England, these bands were influenced by punk rock and hardcore music, hence the 'core' in the name. It is theorize the 'Grind' comes from the guitar pick movements. The music is politically leftist much like Punk music and is focused on speed, and screaming rather than growling.

Clothes Worn & Culture

This genre did not have a certain look necessarily. Due to its punk influences, some members would wear some punk attire staples such as anarchy shirts at times but mostly they wore regular clothes. This genre is known for being the fastest and having the shortest songs.

Fun Facts

This genre is notorious for having the band 'Napalm Death' perform a 5 second song called 'You Suffer'. This won a Guineas World Record for shortest song ever recorded. Additionally, the band 'Carcass' is known for using their medical knowledge for their songs. They use medical and morgue terms to write their songs, making their songs great anatomy lessons.
