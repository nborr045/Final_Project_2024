How It Came to Be

This genre mixes Metalcore with the vocals of Death Metal. It differs from Death Mwtal in that Death Metal will speed up its instrumetals at the height of the song. On the other hand, Deathcore slows down the song and instrumentals so the listener can 'feel the heaviness' more. This is what in metal is caled a 'Breakdown'.

Clothes Worn & Culture

Although this genre did not have an initial specific look or style, it began to attract many people from the Emo subcultures much like in Metalcore. Emo attire was what was more worn by fans and band members alike include hairstyle and accessories. When the breakdown begins in the song, it became popular to do what is called 'crab headbanging'. This consists od squating and banging your head either side-to-side or in circles. One squats so low that they look as if they are doing a crab walk, hence the name. Although this genre did not become Mainstream due to its heaviness, metla fans still looked down upon it due to the amount of Emo fans and their past with Metalcore.

Fun Facts

Bring Me The Horizon was one of the most popular bands in the Deathcore genre and one of the heaviest. Despite this, every album they release becomes less heavy and they are currently an alternative pop band. They have gone mainstream and have even collaborated with artists such as Halsey and Ed Sheeran.
