How It Came to Be

Taking inspiration from Heavy Metal, bands began to produce music with Pop themes. That is, the lyrics had the theme of parties, drugs, romance, etc. The genre focused more on the appearance of the bands rather than the production of the music. Very fun genre. Popular bands include Def Leppard, Whitesnake, Poison, etc.

Clothes Worn & Culture

This genre was sometimes also called 'Hair Metal' as members loved to tease their hair and use a lot of hairspray. The attire usually included spandex, leather, fun patterns like cheetah print, and accessories such as bandanas and fingerless gloves.

Fun Facts

The bassist of Motley Crue, Nikki Sixx, was once at a party and overdosed. He was taken to the hospital and revived. After being discharged from the hospital on the same night, he went back to the party. This genre was big on parties and rebellion.
