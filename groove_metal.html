How It Came to Be

This genre began by bands mizing the vocals from Death metal and mixing them with sounds and instrumentals of Thrash Metal. Older Thrash Metal bands began to release Groove Metal albums as a result. The band that really popularized the genre was Pantera since the lead singer,'Phil Anselmo', had a naturally deep voice and had the deep vocals the band was looking for.

Clothes Worn & Culture

This genre did not have a certain look. Due to its Death and Thrash influences though, many people wore the comfortable attire of Death Metal and the jean vests of Thrash Metal.

Sad Facts

When the band Pantera broke up in the early 2000s, former guitarist Dimebag Darrell joined another band called 'Damage Plan'. In 2004, a shooting broke out at the beggining of their concert in which he was shot and kileld. To this day, this is one of the worst deaths in metal as it was live in front of the crowd.
